Animations

Animation duration

  • Avoid animations that last less than 3 seconds
  • Longer animations allow people more time to perceive the motion and understand what's happening
Recommendation

Motion and flashing

  • Avoid animations that flash more than 3 times per second
  • Ensure animations do not cause seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy
  • Provide an option to disable animations and motion effects
  • Respect the prefers-reduced-motion media query for users who prefer reduced motion

Additional resources:

  • W3 guide on animation
SC 2.3.1 Level A

Parallax and autoplay

  • Use parallax effects sparingly and ensure they don't cause motion sickness
  • Do not autoplay videos or animations on page load
  • Provide user control over media playback
SC 2.2.2 Level A
