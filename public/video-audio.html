Video & Audio

Captions

  • Ensure all videos have captions for dialogue and important sounds
  • Captions must be synchronized with the audio
  • Include sound descriptions for important non-dialogue audio (e.g., "[car engine roaring]")

Additional resources:

  • W3 guide on accessible multimedia
SC 1.2.1 Level A

Audio description

  • Ensure videos have audio descriptions of important visual information
  • Audio descriptions should describe actions, characters, scene changes, and other important visuals
  • Provide either integrated descriptions or a separate audio track
SC 1.2.3 Level A

Transcript

  • Provide transcripts for audio and video content
  • Transcripts should include dialogue and important sound descriptions
  • Place transcripts near the media player or provide a link
Recommendation

Media controls

  • Ensure media players have keyboard accessible controls
  • Provide pause/play, volume, and caption toggle controls
  • All controls should be operable via keyboard
SC 2.1.1 Level A
